About Us

Dedicated to empowering the Telangana diaspora, our organization champions the cause of social empowerment, cultural preservation, and community development. We focus on nurturing sustainable and inclusive growth, ensuring every member of our community has the support and resources they need to thrive.

Our initiatives span from educational empowerment, skill development programs, to providing healthcare and legal aid. We believe in the power of collaboration and community strength to bring about meaningful change and progress in society.

Driven by values of solidarity, compassion, and service, we strive to be a beacon of hope and support for the Pravasis of Telangana. Join us in making a difference, whether through volunteer work, donations, or participating in our various programs.

Mission & Objectives

  1. Fostering Social Empowerment: We pledge to strengthen the bonds of unity, empowering communities to build a secure and harmonious society that celebrates cultural magnificence.
  2. Cultivating National Pride: Our initiatives are designed to kindle a profound sense of nationalism, unifying the diverse tapestry of Indian citizenry in shared loyalty and reverence for our nation.
  3. Pravasi Welfare Enhancement: A special emphasis will be placed on advancing the welfare of Pravasis in Telangana, ensuring they receive our full support and advocacy.
  4. Proactive Health Initiatives: We commit to proactive health education and wellness programs, prioritizing preventive care and the promotion of holistic well-being.
  5. Employment and Skills Development: Recognizing the transformative power of employment, we will implement programs to enhance vocational skills and job readiness, especially among the Pravasis of Telangana.
  6. Sustainable Donor Engagement: We vow to create a sustainable ecosystem of giving, recognizing our donors and fostering a virtuous cycle of generosity and gratitude.
  7. Educational Empowerment: To ignite minds and build futures, we will support educational initiatives that provide access to quality learning for all.
  8. Environmental Stewardship: The Trust will engage in activities that reflect our commitment to environmental conservation, acknowledging our duty to future generations.
  9. Promotion of Arts and Culture: We shall preserve and promote the rich tapestry of our arts and culture, providing platforms for expression and appreciation.
  10. Adaptive and Inclusive Programs: In an ever-changing world, our Trust will remain agile, adopting strategies that address emergent societal needs while maintaining an unwavering commitment to inclusivity and equity.

Telangana Pravasi Samaj Welfare Organization

Empowering communities, fostering unity, and nurturing a sustainable and inclusive future.
